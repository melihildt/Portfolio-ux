Ironcloud Mobile App

Ironcloud is a management system for fitness clubs with two types of app:


  • One for the management for owners and staff gym only
  • Other for the members that allows them to book and cancel classes, view activity history, view the membership information and enter to the gym using a QR code.


From wireframes lo-fi to high-fi

I designed the components of the UI on high fidelity based on wireframes made in Balsamiq, I had in mind a neutral style and a flexible color palette so that it adapts well to the identity of any gym I decided to add a little personality and character with the use of the typography and the shadows.

Final design

This project allowed me to work with Atomic Design, with the minimum possible amount of components to reuse them giving consistency to the complete application and facilitating its further development.
